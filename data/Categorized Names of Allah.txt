Categories of the Names of Allah
Based on the explanations and profound meanings provided in the document "Duas Through Allah’s Names | Laylatul Qadr and the Last 10 Nights," the 118 invocations have been thoughtfully organized into 20 thematic categories.
1. The Supreme & Comprehensive Name
The ultimate anchor encompassing every divine attribute and perfection.
* 86. Ya Allah (The One True God)
* 89. Ya Allah (The Most Merciful)
2. Mercy, Compassion, & Love
Names reflecting His boundless grace, maternal tenderness, and deep affection for His creation.
* 1. Ya Rahman (The Most Merciful)
* 28. Ya Wadud (The Most Loving)
* 50. Ya Latif (The Subtle One)
* 79. Ya Hayyiyy (The Modest / Shy One)
* 90. Ya Rahim (The Bestower of Mercy)
* 111. Ya Ra'uf (The Compassionate)
3. Forgiveness, Pardon, & Concealment
Names invoking His repeated pardoning, acceptance of repentance, and veiling of our flaws.
* 26. Ya Ghaffar (The All-Forgiving)
* 27. Ya Tawwab (The Acceptor of Repentance)
* 40. Ya Sattar (The Concealer of Sins)
* 78. Ya Afuww (The Pardoner)
* 98. Ya Ghafur (The Much-Forgiving)
4. Creation, Origin, & Fashioning
Names highlighting His deliberate, flawless design and origination of life.
* 18. Ya Khaliq (The Creator)
* 19. Ya Badi' (The Originator)
* 20. Ya Musawwir (The Fashioner)
* 21. Ya Mubdi' (The Beginner)
* 93. Ya Bari' (The Maker/Evolver)
5. Majesty, Greatness, & Glory

* 17. Ya Jalil (The Majestic)
* 45. Ya Hamid (The Praiseworthy)
* 71. Ya Kabir (The Great)
* 72. Ya Azim (The Magnificent)
* 74. Ya Majeed (The Glorious)
* 92. Ya Mutakabbir (The Supreme/Majestic)
* 106. Ya Majid (The Noble)
* 113. Ya Dhu-al-Jalal wa-al-Ikram (Lord of Majesty and Generosity)

* 3. Ya Witr (The Singular)

* 44. Ya Ghani (The Self-Sufficient / Free of Need)
* 105. Ya Wajid (The Finder / Lacking Nothing)
* 107. Ya Ahad (The Unique)
